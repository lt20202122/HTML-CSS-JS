Summary

We offer a 2h-course, where we are practicing the old techniques from Japan, called Shinrin-yuko. We will spend 100% of our time in a forest, where we will take a look at insects, small plants and trees.
It is proven that forest bathing has a very positive effect on the human psyche, and reduces stress and anxiety effectively. Many studies have shown, that people, who are frequently doing forest bathing, have a lower amount of cortisol in their bodies than others.
